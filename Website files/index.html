About

This panic alarm is to give vulnerable people the security of knowing that they are a push of a button away from help, with the option of storing multiple phone numbers there is the comfort that more than one person will respond. Once activated your loved ones know youâ€™re in distress and call the necessary authorities, you will be notified that help is on the way.




Mobility

GEMPA is connected to the GSM mobile network, it offers mobility without the constrants of been tied to a fixed basestation


Accelerometer

In the event that someone cannot activate the panic alarm manualy a bulit in Accelerometer will set of the panic alarm when the user falls


GPS

With GPS (Global Positioning System), your location can be sent to your loved ones in the event you cannot give your location .


Remote control

The panic alarm can be activated remotely with a key-fob that can be worn around the neck or a belt buckle.


Visual Aid

The panic alarm gives the family or emergency workers a visual aid at the time when light is fading.


Audible Aid

Along with visual aid, there is a siren that will play a certain tone that will give family or emergency workers an audible aid when line of sight cannot be made


False Alarm

Incase of an accidental activation, this could occur when the Accelerometer sensor is activated, the alarm is fitted with a false alarm button.


Reset

The panic alarm can be reset on site by a first reponder.


Database

With this device you have access to the GEMPA Database, where you can access the Database by logging in below and entering your username and password, there the last know location of the user including time and date is recorded plus all previous activations. These values are subjet to a GPS signal been present. The user current location can be found by clicking on the corrdinates and redirected to Google maps.
